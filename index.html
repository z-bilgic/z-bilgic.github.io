Zeynep Bilgic

Hi there. I am a sophomore at Evanston Township Highschool. I was born in Maryland. I moved to Evanston when I was two years old. Growing up in Evanston I was able to connect with the community in many ways. I often volunteered at Evanston Public Library, and helped out at K-8 chess tournaments held at Evanston schools. I love being able to be apart of my community, whether it be volunteering or participating in community activities with friends and family.

I love sketching and drawing, and am trying to teach myself how to draw digitally. I also have a couple short animations that I worked on. I included a sample of my drawings at my art page. In 2022, I won an art contest in Evanston to spread awareness about the importance of recycling. Recently, however, generative AI is becoming used more and more frequently, especially to replace time-consuming and occasionally expensive human art.

The more I learned about the negative impacts artificial intelligence could have the more I wanted to learn about the positive effects it could have. I completed an AI certificate at Oakton Community College and now know more about machine learning and applications of AI that are less popular commercially. On top of AI, I am also interested in science, technology, math, and chess. I included more information about my education and training in my CV page.

I wrote this page in HTML and CSS using VS Code. I got help from a W3 Schools tutorial on bootstrap, and spent many hours researching and implementing it into this webpage.
